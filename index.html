Changing the Way Business is Played

We live in a society that thrives off our insecurities, one that teaches us to measure ourselves by what we do and have, not who we are. ENOUGH was created to rewire culture and remind us of the truth: our worth is inherent, not earned. We are enough simply because we exist.

This ENOUGH movement is out to spread the word with 100% of profits fund mental wellness organizations, helping transform and save lives while shifting culture toward a world where feeling "enough" becomes the default.

As Mavericks, we're out to "Change the Way Business is Played." We believe that business can and should be the greatest lever for good, so this isn't just another "mastermind." We're bringing Maverick Visionary entrepreneurs together to amplify projects that matter — including yours.

Maverick of Mental Health: Enough

Let's Put Your Brainpower, Talents, Resources and Network to Work for Mental Health Projects

Mavericks are needed more than ever who think different around the Mental Health crisis facing our world. The numbers are striking with nearly 1 in 5 U.S. adults (roughly 60 million people) live with a mental illness (3X more than cancer). Youth mental health is an alarming issue with 15.4% of youth (12–17) experiencing a major depressive episode in 2024. Mavericks are here to make a dent in this issue.
