Featured Maverick of Mental Health

ENOUGH

A Cultural Intervention, Not a Wellness Trend

ENOUGH was created by Blake Mycoskie, the founder of TOMS, after his own battle with depression, anxiety and the relentless pursuit of "more." It exists to rewire the way we define worth and remind people that who they are is already enough.

This is not a brand. It is a movement. ENOUGH tackles the root cause of the mental health crisis: the toxic belief that our value is tied to our output, our success, or what we own.

The Movement

Rewiring Culture,
One Mind at a Time

We live in a society that thrives off our insecurities, one that teaches us to measure ourselves by what we do and have, not who we are. The toll on mental health is staggering and it hits entrepreneurs especially hard. ENOUGH was created to rewire that narrative and remind us of the truth: our worth is inherent, not earned. We are enough simply because we exist.

The ENOUGH movement is out to spread the word, with 100% of profits from all ENOUGH products going to mental health organizations. It's about transforming lives and shifting culture toward a world where feeling "enough" becomes the default. Mental health isn't a topic at this event. It's the reason this event exists.

As Mavericks, we believe that business can and should be the greatest lever for good. So this isn't just another "mastermind." We're bringing Maverick Visionary entrepreneurs together to amplify projects that matter — starting with the most important project of all: the mental wellbeing of everyone in the room and beyond.
